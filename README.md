### Most Optimal Skills to Learn for Data Analyst Roles

<img width="630" height="470" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79a53fe4-e2dd-4ec8-95ad-9030a4413e6b" />

To identify the most optimal skills, this analysis combines both market demand and earning potential by calculating a composite score:

**Optimal Skill Score = Skill Demand √ó Average Salary**

Where:
- **Skill Demand** = number of job postings requiring the skill
- **Average Salary** = average annual salary associated with that skill

SQL and Python emerge as the most optimal skills for Data Analysts in 2023 due to their unmatched combination of high demand and strong salaries. While niche skills can offer higher pay, foundational analytics tools provide the greatest overall market value and career flexibility.
